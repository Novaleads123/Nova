# Nova Leads

## Vision

Nova Leads is an AI-powered B2B lead generation and sales intelligence
platform that helps businesses discover qualified prospects, uncover
business opportunities, and generate personalized outreach.

## Mission

Help businesses spend less time searching for customers and more time
closing deals.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Core Value Proposition

-   Qualified B2B leads
-   Company research
-   Review & sentiment insights
-   AI-generated outreach
-   Freemium model (10 free leads)
-   Credit-based billing
